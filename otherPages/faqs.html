Engraving Information

  1. All the lead-free pewter i.d. bracelets can be engraved on the back. Please keep in mind that less letters on the engraving means bigger letters for emergency medical personnel to read. Here is our sample abbreviation list of some of the more common conditions:
      1. HTN - Hypertension
      2. IDD - Insulin dependent diabetes
      3. IRD - Insulin resistant diabetes
      4. PCN - Penicillin
      5. MEDS - Medication
  2. We recommend that you add "hi", "low", "no" before/after where necessary. Your name and/or emergency contact phone number can also be included.
  3. At check-out, you will be instructed to type exactly what is to be engraved on the back. Please make sure that the spelling is accurate as all engraved pieces are non-returnable.
