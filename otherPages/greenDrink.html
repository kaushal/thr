Green Thrive Energize Drink

1 cucumber with skin on
2 stalks of celery
1 apple, pear, or a half of each
2-3 handfuls of spinach, romaine, or kale or a combination.
1 inch piece of fresh ginger root
1/3 lemon with rind
8 oz water and ice

Place all ingredients into a blender and blend until desired thickness is reached.
