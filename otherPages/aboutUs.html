About the Designer

Lisa McGorry has been a successful aesthetician for the past 25 years. She has been working alongside a plastic surgeon
for 20 years, enabling her to stay on the cutting edge of an ever-evolving billion dollar industry. After Lisa finished her
cancer treatments in 2009, she went on to be one of the first in New Jersey to become a certified oncology aesthetician
by Touch for Cancer, to give back to those going through chemotherapy and radiation treatments.

Lisa designed the Thrive logo and the Thrive medical alert bracelets after the diagnosis becauseshe wanted others to feel
good during difficult times in their lives. She hopes thesestylish, inspirational items will resonate and create positive feelings for the wearer.

Lisa enjoys hiking, running, rollerblading, pilates, yoga, participating in fitness races, green drinks, concocting healthy
recipes,and spending quality times with family and friends. She and her husband Richard have been married since 2005 and
love to travel when they're not too busy with their son, daughter, and three step-children. They reside in Oakland, New Jersey.
